How to host
Become a host with Byn and start making money for your extra space!

  • 1.

    List Your Byn

    List your extra space on Byn! It's free and only takes a few minutes. Provide a photo, describe the space, and set your price! Whether a small closet or an entire garage, you decide how your space gets used.

  • 2.

    Accept Bookings

    Renters will browse Byns and try to book yours. They'll let you know what they're storing, and the dates they need. All you have to do is accept! You can accept multiple bookings for a space if everything fits.

  • 3.

    Coordinate Drop Off

    Communicate with your renter a few days before drop-off to pick a time that works for both of you. On drop-off day, show your renter to their Byn. No awkward cash exchanges, get paid through Byn!
