Byn is a new type of storage company.

Our platform establishes a community marketplace for people to discover new places to store their belongings. We want to revolutionize the storage industry. There is extra space everywhere, Byn gives users the ability to find those spaces and conveniently store their stuff for much cheaper than conventional storage companies. Byn hopes to create new income streams for anyone with extra space, and open up storage to the world!
